Private Coaching

Private sessions are for learners who want focused support and a personal coaching plan. We identify your strengths, build vocabulary, strengthen grammar, and help your English flow in a calm, no-stress setting.

  • Confidence, fluency, grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation
