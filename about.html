My background

Helping ESL learners build confidence for more than 25 years.

For the past 25+ years, I have worked with ESL learners from many countries, helping them develop language skills as they integrate into Canadian culture.

For the past 4 years, my focus has been helping Egyptian learners find fluency. I believe every Egyptian student of English is better than they think.

Let me show you.

My approach

Strengths first, confidence always.

I work with you to identify your strengths and use them to build a stronger vocabulary and better command of your English.

Using a topic-based approach, learners choose subjects we discuss. Your skills are challenged with occasional quizzes to check your growing knowledge, but the environment stays relaxed and encouraging.

What makes the coaching different?

  • Relaxed, no-stress conversation practice
  • Topics chosen around learner interests
  • Confidence building before perfection
  • Grammar and vocabulary taught in useful context
  • Private, group, and specialized support
